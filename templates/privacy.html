Privacy Policy

Your privacy and anonymity are fundamental to Serenity. This policy explains how we protect your information and maintain your anonymity.

Last updated: January 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

Serenity is built on the principle of complete anonymity. We understand that privacy is essential for mental health support, and we've designed our entire platform around protecting your identity and personal information.

  • No Real Names Required: Only anonymous nicknames are used throughout the platform
  • Optional Email: Email addresses are optional and never shared with other users
  • Secure Data Storage: All data is encrypted and stored securely
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell or share your personal data

Information We Collect

Account Information
  • • Anonymous nickname (required)
  • • Email address (optional)
  • • Encrypted password
  • • Account preferences
Wellness Data
  • • Assessment responses
  • • Mood tracking entries
  • • Habit tracking data
  • • Creative content (poems, journals)
Community Interactions
  • • Anonymous chat messages
  • • Community group participation
  • • Shared creative content
Technical Information
  • • Session data (temporary)
  • • Usage analytics (anonymized)
  • • Error logs (for improvements)

How We Use Your Information

Provide Mental Health Support

Deliver personalized wellness tools and community connections based on your assessment results.

Enable Community Features

Match you with appropriate support groups while maintaining complete anonymity.

Track Your Progress

Help you visualize patterns in your mental wellness journey through personal dashboards.

Improve Platform Safety

Moderate content and maintain a safe, supportive environment for all users.

How We Protect Your Data

Encryption & Security
  • • All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • • Secure password hashing
  • • Regular security audits
  • • HTTPS-only connections
Anonymity Protection
  • • No real names or identifying information
  • • Pseudonymous community interactions
  • • No cross-referencing with external data
  • • Optional email addresses

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Share Your Personal Data

Serenity never sells, rents, or shares your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Limited Exceptions:
  • Legal Requirements: Only when required by law or court order
  • Safety Concerns: If we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent harm to you or others
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Privacy Rights

Access Your Data

Request a copy of all personal data we have about you.

Correct Your Data

Update or correct any inaccurate information in your account.

Delete Your Data

Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data.

Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your data while keeping your account active.

Contact Us About Privacy

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us:

Email: serenity1squad@gmail.com

Data Protection Officer: Available upon request

Response Time: We respond to privacy requests within 12 hours

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • We'll notify you through the platform or email
  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • You'll have the opportunity to review changes

Questions About Your Privacy?

We're committed to transparency and protecting your anonymity. If you have any concerns or questions about how we handle your data, please don't hesitate to reach out.
